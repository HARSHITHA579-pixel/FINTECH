Our Infrastructure

1. Quality Assurance Lab

Our dedicated Quality Room includes:

  • 10+ Mitutoyo measuring tools: Micrometers (0–100 mm), Digital and Dial Verniers, and Bore Gauges
  • Digital Height Gauge (0-300 mm)
  • Surface plate (2000 x 1000 x 150 mm)
  • Magnetic V-Blocks for cylindrical workholding

2. Supporting Equipment & Tooling

A wide range of auxiliary machines and custom tooling supports operations such as:

  • Fixture building
  • Jig assembly
  • Manual operations
  • DRO Milling Machine
  • Sub-assemblies and pre-production setups
  • 3D printer for select prototyping tasks

3. Prototyping & SPM Expertise

We specialize in developing prototype parts and Special Purpose Machines (SPMs), tailored to client needs in automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation sectors.

4. High-Mix, Low-Volume & Export Readiness

Our infrastructure is optimized for high-mix, low-volume production and is equipped for direct export of machined components to the USA, Canada, France, and China—serving customers like Ingersoll Rand, Kindred AI, Moog, TE Connectivity, Amphenol, Cepheid, DRDO-GTRE and ICAM Solutions.
